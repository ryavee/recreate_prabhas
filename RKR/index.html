About Coaching

There is no right or wrong way to get there. Your path is your path.

You will learn how your brain works, why you are uniquely the way you are and what steps you can take to make subtle, yet significant changes that will benefit your life and your relationships. My goal for you is that you end up feeling like you have received immeasurable value. That your own life and your relationships are content and fulfilled. Your decision to choose to enhance your life through having a coach becomes an investment and not an expense.

My coaching is experiential, engaging and promotes lasting growth and development. You can’t unlearn what you are taught. You can decide not to use the tools and return to mediocrity. But you can’t unlearn it.

Services

Hiring a coach is a decision to invest in yourself.

You are the most important asset in your environment.

Clients initially enroll for a 12 week session. Sessions are 60-75 minutes via telephone. Once we agree on your session day and time, we are both committed to the same time every week.

Then most clients choose to re-enroll for another 12 sessions, or decide to continue month to month for a year.

I offer you an initial complimentary coaching session to get to learn more about each other and learn how being coached will benefit you.

Contact me today to find out more and schedule your first complimentary session.

Ravi Raut is President and Founder of Raut Coaching. In his 5 years of coaching he has been consistently recognized for his coaching style, clear communication skills, and the results her clients achieve. Ravi Raut is a champion for creating powerful experiences and results that leave a lasting impact and connection with her clients.

Throughout the coaching process, he challenges and teaches individuals, couples, and business executives to look beyond the obvious and identify barriers as well as recognize all possibilities to achieve success. Coach Raut is engaging, thought-provoking and insightful, guiding you to think beyond the expected and consider various angles to define your personal and professional purpose. He works closely with his clients to organize a plan of action with a focus on fulfilling professional and personal goals and dreams.
